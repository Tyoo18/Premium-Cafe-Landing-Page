Our Manifesto

Handcrafted to
restore your pace.

We began in Tokyo with a single idea: that the physical spaces we occupy dictate our mental speeds. Kinto is not designed for transaction; it is crafted for presence. Every piece of Douglas fir wood was reclaimed from historic storefronts; every stoneware cup was thrown by local artisans.

Our beans are sourced directly from small micro-lots in southern Ethiopia and northern Kyoto, roasted in-house in small, unhurried batches each Tuesday morning.

Kento Yamazaki Founder & Maker
